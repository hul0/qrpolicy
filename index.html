QR Code Scanner & Generator - QRWiz

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how QR Code Scanner & Generator - QRWiz ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

QR Code Scanner & Generator - QRWiz is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data or usage information from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • • Your QR code scan history or generated QR code content. All scanning and generation processes occur locally on your device.
  • • Your device's unique identifiers (beyond what third-party ad services may automatically collect, as described below).
  • • Any information about your precise geographical location.

2. Local Data Storage

The App utilizes local storage (specifically, Android's SharedPreferences) on your device to manage certain app-specific settings and features. This locally stored data includes:

  • • Your coin balance for the in-app gamification system.
  • • Your dark mode preference.
  • • Other non-personal app settings necessary for the App's functionality.

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties. If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data will be removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. These third-party ad services operate independently and may collect information directly from your device according to their own privacy policies. This collection is necessary for them to provide relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Google AdMob:

Google AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google LLC. For more information on how Google collects, uses, and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Please note: While we do not collect your data, the advertising networks integrated into the App may use technologies such as cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect data about your device and your interests to serve personalized advertisements.

4. Permissions

QR Code Scanner & Generator - QRWiz requires the following permissions to function:

  • • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): This permission is essential for the QR code scanning functionality. Without it, the scanner cannot operate.
  • • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET): This permission is used exclusively for displaying advertisements (via Google AdMob) and for validating coin rewards when you scan a QR code (to prevent fraud). The core QR scanning functionality works completely offline.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data, we implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information stored locally on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

QR Code Scanner & Generator - QRWiz is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
